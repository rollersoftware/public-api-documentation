# Creating Customer Records in ROLLER

[**Customer records**](../reference/reporting-api.yaml/paths/~1data~1customers/get) are created in ROLLER via two primary methods:

1. Via a booking, as the booking holder.
2. By signing a waiver, or having a waiver signed on their behalf (in the case of minors).
