# Creation of Customer Records in ROLLER

[**Customer records**](https://roller.stoplight.io/docs/roller-api/reference/Data%20API/DataAPI.yaml/paths/~1data~1customers/get) are created in ROLLER via two primary methods.

> 1. Via a booking as the booking holder
> 
> 2. By signing a waiver or having a waiver signed on their behalf (minors)
